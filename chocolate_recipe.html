Chocolate Cake

"The Chocolate Cake recipe is a classic. This version of the recipe is fast and quick and your kids will love it."

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200 g

Sugar: 150 g

Butter: 200 g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80 g

Instructions

1

 Melt the chocolate and the butter together

2

 Mix the sugar, eggs and the flour

3

 Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

 Bake in the oven
